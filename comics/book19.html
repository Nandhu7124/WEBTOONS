DEMON SLAYER



The Demon Slayer manga is worth reading, for fans of the anime and newbies alike. I should also add a quick postscript for budding fans who have yet to watch the anime because they wanted to check out the Kimetsu no Yaiba manga first. That postscript can be slimmed down to a simple recommendation: read Demon Slayer. It is an absolute pleasure. Demon Slayer is one of the few stories, in manga or anime form, that can be enjoyed by anyone. It is a perfect manga and a perfect anime. Wherever you choose to start, know that you’ll have a great time. You’ll laugh, cry, scream, sigh, and feel elated the entire time.
